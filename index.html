About Me

Hello, my name is Alexandra Gonzalez

I just moved to the area and am a cosmetologist currently working at Hair Affair & Co in Cle Elum. I studied at Gary Manuel Aveda Institute in Seattle; where I learned many hair cutting and coloring techniques; as well as natural nail manicures and pedicures.
Hair and nails not only change how you look, but also how you feel, and I am excited to work with you. As hair trends are constantly changing, I make it my goal to keep up with what's new to make sure I meet all your hair wants and needs.
